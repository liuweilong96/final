Introduction

What is 'One Piece'

Welcome! This is a website that made by a fan and is trying to make more people get to know 'One Piece' and enjoy the fun of this long but interesting japanese manga. This website will contain four meaningful pages includes this one: the intro page (this page), the page about the author, the page about the history of the born of 'One Piece' and a page about some famous characters. 'One Piece' is one of the most famous Japanese manga and is also one of the oldest one which keeps releasing new story and new manga book. This manga starts with a boy called Monkey D. Luffy who inspired by "Red Haired" Shanks and got a dream of being the king of pirate. The story goes with how Luffy meets new friends, fight against enemies and keep chasing his dream. Untill now, 'One Piece' is still releasing new story for about 20 years and all of our fans are looking forward of more fantastic adventure and hope Luffy can make his dream come true.
